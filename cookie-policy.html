Cookie Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit to make your next visit easier and the site more useful to you.

2. How MacroLog Uses Cookies

MacroLog uses only one functional cookie, stored in your browser's localStorage:

  • macrolog_cookie_consent — Records that you have accepted this cookie policy so the consent banner does not reappear on subsequent visits. This is a strictly necessary functional cookie and cannot be disabled without affecting site functionality.

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking cookies, or any third-party analytics cookies.

3. Third-Party Services

MacroLog loads fonts from Google Fonts via a CDN. Google may set its own cookies or collect standard request data (such as your IP address) when serving font files. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect how the site functions. To reset your consent preference on MacroLog, clear your browser's localStorage for this site.

Most browsers allow you to:

  • View which cookies are stored
  • Delete cookies individually or in bulk
  • Block cookies from specific or all websites

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

6. Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact the developer through the University of Iowa student directory or the MacroLog GitHub repository.
